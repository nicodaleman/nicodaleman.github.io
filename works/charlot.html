The artists present improvisations on a multi-channel audiovisual system conceived specifically for the auditorium of the Charlot Theater. Part sound installation, part jam-session, the system will be open to collaboration, where the acoustic perception will take us on a journey of new possible and impossible worlds through the manipulation of sonic material in space.
