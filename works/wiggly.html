Wiggly is a multichannel performance in which spatial sound perception gets altered by the psychoacoustical phenomena inherent to our listening. Different sound sources with subtle algorithmic-driven variations of spatial placements, frequency and spectra archive this effect while creating an unstable state of sonic material.
