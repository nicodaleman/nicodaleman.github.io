--- layout: workpage title: Dualities ---

Multimedia performance for a dancer, 3D sound system, motion tracker, binaural headphone rendering and live electronics.

2019

This piece aims to explore the possibilities of immersive technologies in the realm of sound. Our aim is to take advantage of the possibilities of visually immersive realities and create a piece that engages mostly with the sonic component of them, namely, binaural and 3D audio, through the interaction with body gestures, creating a form of interaction that privileges the sonic over the visual through embodied listening. These interactions will ultimately pose questions about the reality of our perception: We will not ask ‘what is reality?’ but rather ‘what is sonic reality?’
