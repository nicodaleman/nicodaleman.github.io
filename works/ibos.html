Collaborative Duo with Dr. Shama Rahman for Sitar and Electronics

2021

Brain.wav brings unique process iBoS (Input Brain Output Sound) improvising with both brain and acoustic waves, creating biosonic loops. Through digital signal processes Nico captures and sonifies Shama's brainwaves into electronica sounds, while Shama incorporates the sonified brainwaves into her improvisation, generating a recursive loop – and of course, listening to her own brainwaves changes her own brainwaves, so it’s a tightrope act.

Nico Daleman · Brain.wav (Collaborative Duo with Dr. Shama Rahman)
