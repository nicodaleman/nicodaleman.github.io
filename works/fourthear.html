--- layout: workpage title: Fourth Ear ---

For 8-channel system, headphones, head-tracker, midi controller and laptop (Max8)

2019

Fourth Ear is a piece that explores embodied listening through two simultaneous techniques: Distortion Produced Otoacoustic Emissions (DPOAE) and headphone binaural rendering controlled via headtracking. The objective is to take advantage of the possibilities of visually immersive realities and create a piece that engages mostly with the sonic component of them, namely, binaural and multichannel audio. The relationship with body gestures creates a form of interaction that privileges the sonic over the visual through embodied listening.
