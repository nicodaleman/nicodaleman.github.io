About

Nico Daleman is a Colombian-born musicologist and sound artist currently based in Berlin.

His pieces have been shown in Haus der Kulturen der Welt, CLB at Aufbau Haus, SoundSAbout, modular+space at Greenhouse, among others and he has taken part in performances at Zentrum für Kunst und Medien in Karlsruhe, Silent Green Berlin, Darmstadt 2018, ACUD MACHT NEU and Loophole among many others.

His writtings have been published in MusikText, Positionen and the Berliner Festpiele Blog

He is the host of "The Rest is Music" a monthly show that airs on Cashemre Radio.

Currently his a researcher in the Data Sonification Initiative at the Hasso Plattner Institute in Potsdam.

Nico studied Audio Engineering, Musicology and Sound Studies and Sonic Arts in Bogotá, Boston and Berlin.
